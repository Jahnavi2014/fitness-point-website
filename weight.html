Weight Training

Build Muscle Strength

Weight Training is focused on building muscle strength and endurance using resistance exercises, free weights, barbells, and machines. Regular sessions help you gain strength, tone your body, and improve overall health.

Workout Details

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes per session
  • Frequency: 3-4 times per week
  • Targeted Muscles: Arms, chest, back, legs, and core
  • Benefits: Increased muscle mass, improved posture, enhanced metabolism, and strength for daily activities
