Boxing Training

Enhance Agility & Stamina

Boxing training improves your coordination, reflexes, and endurance. It includes bag training, footwork practice, and high-energy drills.

Training Focus

  • Punching techniques
  • Footwork & movement drills
  • Bag workouts & sparring
  • Core strengthening exercises

Benefits

  • Increases agility and speed
  • Boosts stamina and endurance
  • Excellent full-body workout
  • Enhances focus and mental strength
