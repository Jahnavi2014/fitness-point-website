Cardio Strength

Full-Body Cardio Workout

Cardio Strength workouts are designed to improve your stamina, endurance, and cardiovascular health. These exercises combine running, jumping, and bodyweight routines for maximum calorie burn and overall fitness.

Workout Details

  • Duration: 30-45 minutes per session
  • Frequency: 4-5 times per week
  • Targeted Muscles: Heart, legs, core, arms
  • Benefits: Burns calories, increases endurance, boosts metabolism, and improves heart health
