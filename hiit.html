HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

Short Bursts of Intense Exercise

HIIT involves fast-paced bursts of intense exercise followed by short recovery periods. It burns calories quickly, improves endurance, and boosts metabolism for hours after your workout.

What You’ll Do

  • High-intensity intervals (20–40 seconds)
  • Short rest periods (10–20 seconds)
  • Full-body cardio + strength routines
  • Beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels

Benefits

  • Burns fat faster than normal workouts
  • Improves heart health
  • Boosts stamina and endurance
  • Perfect for busy schedules
